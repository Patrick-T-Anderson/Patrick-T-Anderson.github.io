    A Supply Chain Game

# Goal
The local production of all essential Goods and Services.

# Value Types
INPUTS: Land, Work
OUTPUTS: Goods, Services

# Legal Instruments
DEED: Expiring access to a specific portion of Land. [INPUT]
CONTRACT: Expiring commitment to fulfill a specific kind of future Work. [INPUT]
TICKET: Expiring coupon redeemable for a specific kind of future Good or Service. [OUTPUT]

# Monetary Instruments
COIN: Non-expiring fungible token limited in supply by the number of square meters of land held under these terms.

# Social Agreements
BASIC_OUTCOME: A bundle of TICKETS representing the essential Goods and Services every individual must have, as defined by this Group.

# How to Play
PAY-TO-PLAY: Buy COINS to fund production.

SIGN-TO-STAY: Sign a CONTRACT for a BASIC_OUTCOME of essential Goods and Services.  This implicitly trades your future Work for the future Work of others.

WORK-TO-OWN: Fulfill a CONTRACT to receive DEEDS of Land ownership "similar to" the Land used to fulfill the BASIC_OUTCOME you already received.

OWN-FOR-GOOD: Hold Land DEEDS to own the future Goods provided by that Land.
